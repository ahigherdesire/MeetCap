Questions

What happens after the 7-day trial?

MeetCap keeps running, but recording is paused until you enter a license key. Buy once, paste your key in Settings → License, and you're set.

How do I get my license key?

After you pay through Stripe, we email your unique key to the address on your receipt. Each key is one-of-a-kind.

Is my data private?

Yes. Recordings are saved only on your computer and are never uploaded. Detection uses safe local signals and never reads your meeting content.

What are the system requirements?

Windows 10 or 11 (64-bit). Everything needed is bundled in the installer — no extra downloads.

Do I need to ask people before recording?

Yes — please get consent from participants as required by the laws in your area. MeetCap shows a clear indicator while recording.
