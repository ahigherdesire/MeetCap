Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026. Your privacy matters. Here's exactly what MeetCap does and doesn't do.

Your recordings stay on your device

MeetCap records and stores everything locally on your computer. Your recordings are never uploaded, transmitted, or accessed by us. We have no servers that receive your video or audio.

Meeting detection

Detection uses safe local signals (running apps, window titles, and whether the microphone is in use). It never reads the contents of your meetings and never sends anything over the network.

Information we process

  • Payment & email: When you buy, Stripe processes your payment and shares your email and order details with us so we can send your license key. We use your email only to deliver your key and provide support.
  • License key: Stored locally on your device to unlock recording. Activation works offline; we do not track your usage.

Third parties

We use Stripe for payments. Please review their privacy policy for how they handle payment data.

Questions about privacy? Email us.
